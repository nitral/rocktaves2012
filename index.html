About

Rocktaves is the annual semi-professional rock festival of BITS Pilani. It is without a doubt one of the most prestigious rock competitions in India. Rocktaves is a major event of the cultural festival Oasis, which also includes other musical events such as the solo guitar contest called "Axetacy", and the acoustic unplugged event called "Oasis Unplugged".

For decades, Rocktaves has strived towards excellence, pioneering and redefining the fairly young and volatile Indian rock scene. Rocktaves is, in fact, responsible for launching some of the biggest names in the Indian rock scene today; Parikrama, Prestorika, Them Clones, The Superfuzz and Euphoria are just some of the footnotes in this epic musical odyssey. It just might be your turn now...

Rules

  • Deadline for submission of entries is Friday, 12th October 2012, 23:59hrs.
  • The competition consists of two rounds - Prelims and Finals. Bands will be shortlisted based on solely on the entries they have submitted, and will be called for the Prelims (held during Oasis, at BITS-Pilani in Pilani, Rajasthan).
  • Short listed bands will be informed well in time about the prelims schedule and must report at least 24 hours before the scheduled time.
  • Bands will get 25 minutes on stage (including sound check).
  • There will be no language restrictions on the entries.
  • No registration fees will be charged; accommodation will be provided.
  • The selection of teams will be the sole discretion of BITS-Pilani.
  • Professionals will be handling the judging of the event.
  • Professional sounds shall handle the event. Drum kits will be provided.
  • Any team violating any of the above rules is subject to direct elimination.
  • The final decision regarding any matter lies in the hands of the organizers and judges and is absolutely binding.

Update: The deadline for submission of entries has been extended to Tuesday, 16th October, 2012, 23:59hrs.
