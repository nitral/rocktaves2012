About

"The sudden rush of blood to your head wakes you up with a kick. The bright lights push you forward. The crazed crowd pulls you further. You look around yourself in a daze. You're on a stage that most others only dream of. This is Rocktaves 2011.

p>Rocktaves is the rock festival of BITS Pilani. It is without a doubt one of the most prestigious rock competitions in India. Rocktaves is a major event of the cultural festival Oasis, which also includes other events such as a solo guitar contest called Axetacy, and an acoustic unplugged event called Oasis Unplugged.

For decades, Rocktaves has enjoyed its lofty position as one of the most venerated of all platforms in the fairly nascent Indian rock scene. Its claims to fame are numerous. Rocktaves is in factresponsible for launching some of the biggest names in the Indian rock scene today: Parikrama, Prestorika, Them Clones, The Superfuzz and Euphoria are just some of those names.

You could be next.

Rules

  • Bands need to send in their demo tapes (mp3's) (preferably 4 demos) and band profile. Youtube links can be added. There should be at least two self-compositions in the entry.
  • Deadline for submission is Sunday, 9th October 2011, 0000hrs
  • Short listed bands will be informed well in time about the prelims schedule and must report at least 24 hours before the scheduled time.
  • Professional sounds to handle the event. Drum kits will be provided
  • Professionals will judge the event
  • Bands will get 25 minutes on stage (including sound check).
  • FINAL decision regarding any matter lies in the hands of the organizers and judges and is absolutely binding.
  • No registration fees will be charged and accommodation will be provided.
  • Bands should mail in their entries to the email addresses below: (There should be at least two own compositions in the entry)
  • There will be NO language restrictions on the entries.
  • Any team violating ANY of the above rules is subject to direct elimination.
  • The selection of teams will be the sole discretion of BITS- PILANI
  • The decision of the organizers will be final and binding

Participate

  • Bands need to send in their demos as attachments (preferably mp3's).
  • Bands need to send in least two original compositions.
  • A .pdf .doc file which has the band profile, contact details, past performances etc would be useful.
  • Links to the band's Facebook/Myspace page, youtube links of live performances, photographs, etc are encouraged.
  • Send in your entries to samarth@bits-oasis.org before 9th October 2011 0000 hrs.
  • Shortlisted bands will be invited to come and perform at Rocktaves, Oasis 2011, at BITS Pilani.
